Main Dishes

The primary dish of every meal. Do be careful though, coz' there might be ghosts living under that plate. But do not worry for a discount in Wangsheng Funeral Parlor is given for free.

Suspicious Sweet Madame

Honey-roasted fowl. The sweet flowers have been burnt off, revealing the bitter taste of the overcooked fowl.

Suspicious Jueyun Chili Chickens

Chicken in a dressing, served cold. The chicken probably isn't properly marinated, so it's only salty on the outside but bland on the inside. The taste reminds you of water-poached chicken breasts.

Suspicious Matsutake Meat Rolls

A pan-fried meat dish. Though the dry and tough texture is miles away from what it could be, the Matsutake is too nutritious to let it go to waste.
